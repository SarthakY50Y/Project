Our Universe

Overview

The universe includes everything that exists: space, time, matter, and energy.
It began about 13.8 billion years ago with the Big Bang.
Since then, the universe has been expanding.

Composition

Ordinary matter (stars, planets, humans): ~5%
Dark matter (invisible, gravitational effect): ~27%
Dark energy (causes accelerated expansion): ~68%
